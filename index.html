Welcome to Henderson Country Club

Henderson Country Club carries the kind of quiet history most modern courses would pay good money to fake. A classic 9-hole layout stretching 2,958 yards from the longest tees, it plays to a par 35 and has been part of the Henderson, Texas landscape since 1933. The course offers a relaxed but honest round of golf, the kind where the trees remember your swing and the fairways don’t particularly care about your excuses. Golfers can expect a straightforward experience with approachable green fees around $30 for 9 holes, plus an on-site driving range for loosening up or quietly negotiating with your slice before the round begins. It’s not flashy. It’s not trying to be. It just is—steady, local, and still standing after nearly a century of golfers trying to outsmart it.
